Welcome to Bakes by Srishti, where tradition meets modern baking. Our bakery is born from a love of crafting exceptional treats with creativity and passion. Every bite is made to be an experience, designed to delight and satisfy your cravings.

At Bakes by Srishti, we offer a thoughtfully curated menu of freshly baked breads, pastries, cakes, and cookies. Using the finest ingredients, each item is handmade with care and commitment to quality. From crusty sourdough loaves to buttery croissants and signature chocolate ganache cakes, every creation is crafted to burst with flavor.

Our bakery is more than just food; it's about community. We believe in the power of great food to bring people together, mark milestones, and brighten everyday moments. Every treat we bake carries the joy and warmth of home, meant to create lasting memories with every bite.

Whether you're stopping by for a morning pastry, finding the perfect cake for a celebration, or treating yourself to something sweet, we invite you to experience the love we put into every creation. Bakes by Srishti is a personal labor of love, where every story begins with care.
